We the DEPolitik

“Where the truth and only the truth matters”

DEPolitik is an anonymous, disinterested, non-for profit and collaborative research forum that seeks to expose the world’s truth. With the same genesis as other modern information ideas such as the World Wide Web, Google, and Wikipedia, DEpolitik was conceived out of a belief that transparency of information only makes the world a better place. Hence, DEpolitik unequivocally rejects the hypothesis that in darkness there is happiness.
